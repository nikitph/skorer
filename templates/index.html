  • What is Skorr??

    Cricket happens to be an awesome game. Its a religion for a bunch (Around 2 Billion) of people. Budding cricketers play on small roads and grounds in thousands of matches all over the globe. What Skorr aims is to ease scoring of such impromptu matches. All you need is a smartphone and you are on. In our beta phase we will start by tracking batsman stats. Our apologies to the bowlers. We promise to support you soon.

  • What we are not:

    We don't plan to be a cricket stats database for international/first class/state level matches. Our focus is strictly local.
